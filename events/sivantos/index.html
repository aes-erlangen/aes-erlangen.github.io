WSAudiology - Wonderful Sound for All

February 8, 2023 ◦ 13:45 - 15:45 ◦ Henri-Dunant-Str. 100, Erlangen

We have another fantastic AES event for you before the finals. We are inviting you to the WSAudiology (Sivantos) event. WSAudiology is an industry leader hearing aid company with their motto of "Wonderful Sound for All". With this event, you will get a chance to meet team leaders from different groups of WSA and learn about their tasks. Moreover, you will experience their actual work environment with demo sessions at their 4 different labs!
