AES Student Section now officially registered

7 November 2022 ◦ 1 min read

We are happy to announce that the Student Section Erlangen-Nürnberg is now an accepted member of the AES. You can find us now at aes.org/sections/view.cfm?section=415.

We enjoyed a dinner at Palmyra to celebrate our official recognition. See you all at the next event!
